StuyTown is proud to provide impeccable and unique amenities for our residents with an 80-acre private park right next to the East River.

What Are No-Fee Apartments?

No-fee apartments are properties in which you do not have to pay a real estate broker a commission for helping you find an apartment. You work directly with the property to find your new apartment. No-Fee apartments means no brokers, and no broker fees.

Why Are No Broker Fee Apartments Advantageous?

No-fee NYC apartment rentals actually end up being a good deal for both parties involved. It is advantageous for this approach to be used by certain buildings because for every month an apartment is not being leased by an occupant, the landlord loses money. This also makes the properties attractive to those looking for a place to live because it is less expensive, and therefore the landlord is likely to attract more business.

 

This is obviously advantageous for you, the renter, because you save money. A broker's fee can range anywhere from a full month's rent to 15% of the cost of a full year's lease.

Why Doesn't Everyone Opt for No-Fee Apartment?

No fee apartments can be hard to find. Brokers can seem like the easy, albeit expensive, way to go. But now you can have it all. StuyTown Apartments are "no fee rentals" located in the heart of Manhattan, just walking distance away from great neighborhoods like Gramercy Park, Flatiron District, Union Square, East Village, Murray Hill, Williamsburg, Kips Bay, NYU and the Lower East Side.

Take advantage of the StuyTown amenities offered in its "no-fee" apartments like basketball courts, roller hockey court, ice rink, community center, paddle tennis courts, bocce ball, parking, playgrounds, concierge service, green market, day care center, Enterprise car rentals, and more!
